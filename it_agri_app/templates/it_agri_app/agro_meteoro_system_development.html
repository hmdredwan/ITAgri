With its connections to various disciplines of research such as physical sciences, atmospheric sciences, agricultural sciences, and other sciences, agrometeorology has a broad scope. The study of agricultural sciences would be incomplete without consideration of agrometeorological knowledge and data. The goal of agrometeorology is to identify meteorological, climatological, and hydrological issues and apply this information to agricultural applications. Agrometeorology studies cover a wide range of topics, from the soil surface layer to the depths to which roots can penetrate, and from the atmosphere to the heights at which plants live and thrive. For optimal crop growth, specific climatic conditions are essential. As a result, it tries to improve agricultural productivity by managing the physical environment, forecasting weather accurately, and estimating crop yields. IT Agri has its expertise to develop Agrometeorology System for institution, organisation etc.
